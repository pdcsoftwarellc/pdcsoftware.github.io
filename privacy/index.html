Privacy Policy

Air Pong: Rivals is designed to be played without giving us your data.

Effective July 17, 2026

The short version

The app does not collect your personal information.

Air Pong: Rivals has no user accounts, advertising, analytics, tracking, or online gameplay. PDC Software does not receive your gameplay data.

01 Information we collect

The Air Pong: Rivals app does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. It does not request access to your contacts, location, photos, camera, or microphone.

02 Data stored on your device

The app stores game progress, settings, records, and unlocked content locally on your device. This information is used only to provide the game experience. PDC Software cannot access it.

Your device's operating-system backup service may include locally stored app data according to your device and backup settings. Deleting the app removes its local data from the device.

03 Third-party services

The app does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services. It does not send gameplay or personal data to PDC Software or third-party servers.

04 Children's privacy

Because the app does not collect personal information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

05 Information you provide for support

If you choose to contact us through GitHub, the information you submit is handled by GitHub under its own privacy terms and may be publicly visible. Please do not include passwords, account credentials, or other sensitive personal information in a support request.

06 Changes to this policy

If the game's data practices change, this policy will be updated and the effective date above will be revised. Material changes will be reflected before the related app update is released.

07 Contact

Questions about this policy or Air Pong: Rivals can be sent through our public support page.
